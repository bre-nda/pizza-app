OUR HISTORY

The Instant Pizza Place was formed in the year 2001 by the late Mr.Daniel Joseph.The late Mr.Daniel loved pizza so much,he used to try different pizza recipes at his home,and he decided to open up restaurant. The restaurant has grown and it is known country wide. We offer the world's greatest and amazing pizza in all varieties. With our italian twist,you wouldn't want to be left behind.
